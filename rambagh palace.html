Rambagh Palace

About Rambagh Palace

Rambagh Palace, located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India, is one of the most luxurious heritage hotels in the world. Initially built as a royal residence in 1835, it was later converted into a hotel in 1957 by the Taj Hotels Resorts and Palaces group.

The palace boasts stunning architecture, lush gardens, and opulent interiors that reflect the grandeur of the bygone era. It offers world-class hospitality and amenities, making it a favorite destination for tourists and dignitaries alike.

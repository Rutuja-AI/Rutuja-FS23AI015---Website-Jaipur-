Gaitore

About Gaitore

Gaitore is a historical site in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India, known for its royal cenotaphs. It is located amidst the picturesque surroundings of the Aravalli Hills and serves as the final resting place for the members of the royal family of Jaipur.

The cenotaphs at Gaitore are intricately designed and showcase the architectural finesse of the Rajput rulers. Each cenotaph has a distinct design and represents the unique style of the ruler it commemorates.

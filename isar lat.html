Isar Lat

About Isar Lat

Isar Lat, also known as the Sargasuli Tower, is a historic monument located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. Built in the 18th century by Maharaja Ishwari Singh, it served as an astronomical observatory.

The tower stands tall at a height of 27 meters and offers panoramic views of the surrounding cityscape. It is adorned with intricate carvings and architectural details, reflecting the grandeur of Rajput architecture.

Main Points

Welcome to Jaipur!

This website is your guide to exploring the rich heritage and culture of Jaipur city.

History of Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, India, was founded by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II on 18 November 1727. It is known as the Pink City due to the distinct color of its buildings. Jaipur is renowned for its rich history, culture, and architectural marvels.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Jaipur is during the winter season, from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and suitable for exploring the city's attractions. Avoid visiting during the summer months (April to June) due to scorching heat.
